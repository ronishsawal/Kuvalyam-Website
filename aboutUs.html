About Us

Sumit Ahuja

Director

Sumit Ahuja’s is a tale of turning his dreams into reality. He had humble beginnings as a site supervisor yet craved fulfilment in his career to reflect his dreams. One fortunate day, he noticed a lapse in the real estate industry and decided to make his mark in it. He devoted himself to acquiring technical and practical knowledge behind building homes. A keen eye for design and a sense of what makes luxury are intrinsic to his personality. And so, Kuvalyam was born, out of the instinct of giving his patrons a second home that is a true escape from busy, urban spaces. It is his promise of bestowing luxury with modern opulence that lies at the heart of every Kuvalyam home!
